How To Play

Journey through the galaxy with our action-packed space shooter game. Choose from multiple levels with increasing difficulty to challenge your skills. Are you ready to save the universe?

The controls are A and D to rotate the ship left and right respectively, E (mouse scroll up) and R (mouse scroll down) can be used to make the ship move up and down. The spacebar must be used in order to shoot the enemies.

The game is quite simple, the objective is to kill the enemy spaceships and the enemy spacestations in the game world.

As mentioned above, there are three levels to the game with increasing difficulty as you progress through them.

The game (level) is won when all the enemy space stations or bases as well as all the already spawned enemy spaceships are eliminated.

Be careful because the enemy spaceships keep spawning at their respective bases until one destroys the enemy station base

Enemy spaceships possess the ability to phase through, i.e enemy spaceships explode if they collide with anything except for other enemy spaceships and their own enemy bases

Make sure to avoid bumping into some things because if you collide with an asteroid, enemy spaceship, enemy bases, planets or moons then you lose the game

Colliding with earth would mean you chose flight instead of fight and ran away from the fight hence the game would be over

If you run away from the game area (a certain long distance in any direction) then you lose and the game is over

Lastly, there are some cool powerups in the game with unique abilities, check them out!

We hope you enjoy Big Theta's 3D Spaceshooter game, Good Luck!
